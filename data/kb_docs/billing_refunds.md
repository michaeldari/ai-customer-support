# AcmeDesk Billing: Refunds & Credits

## When refunds apply
- Duplicate charges
- Cancellation before renewal
- Extended service outage impacting paid users

## How to request a refund
1. Provide invoice number (INV…) and the charge date
2. Include the last 4 digits of the card (do not share full card numbers)
3. We typically process refunds within **5–10 business days**

## Notes
- Taxes (VAT) may be adjusted depending on billing address and tax ID.
